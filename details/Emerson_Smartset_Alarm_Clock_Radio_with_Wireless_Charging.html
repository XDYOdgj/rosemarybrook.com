Emerson Smartset Alarm Clock Radio

Wake up to a smarter routine with the Emerson Smartset Alarm Clock Radio. Designed with a sleek and modern look, this multi-functional device brings convenience, style, and innovation to your home. Featuring a wireless charging pad that supports up to 5W, you can charge your phone and smartwatch effortlessly while you sleep. The built-in temperature sensor lets you monitor your room's climate, keeping things comfortable and in check. A 1.4-inch red LED display with adjustable brightness ensures easy reading, even in low light. Whether you're a morning person or sharing a room with someone, the dual alarm with customizable tones gives you the flexibility to set your own wake-up rhythm. With the patented SmartSet system, the clock automatically adjusts time settings when plugged in or after power outages, including Daylight Saving Time, so you never have to fiddle with the clock again. It's the perfect all-in-one solution for your bedroom or living room, combining functionality and convenience.
