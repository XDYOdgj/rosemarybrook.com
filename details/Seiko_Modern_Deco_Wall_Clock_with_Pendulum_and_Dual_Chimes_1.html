Seiko Modern Deco Wall Clock

Elevate your decor with the Seiko Modern Deco Wall Clock, a striking blend of contemporary and classic styles. This wall clock features a sleek metal pendulum that adds a touch of refinement, while the rose gold wooden veneer and brown accents bring warmth and sophistication to any space.

Equipped with a quartz crystal movement, it delivers precise timekeeping without the need for winding, ensuring reliability and convenience. The dual chime functionality, offering both hourly and quarter-hour alerts, keeps you in tune with the time in a subtle and elegant way. Plus, the automatic chime silencer ensures the clock remains quiet during the night, making it perfect for bedrooms or peaceful living areas.

With an adjustable chime volume, you can customize the sound to suit your personal preference and lifestyle. Whether you're enhancing your living room or adding charm to your kitchen, this clock is designed to complement your interior while delivering both style and accuracy.
